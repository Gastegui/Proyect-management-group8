Hello everyone, and welcome to Mondragon University (MU), located in the beautiful Basque Country, Spain! We are excited to have you here as part of your Erasmus experience.


Who are we

Mondragon University is a renowned institution known for its innovative educational model and strong ties to the industry. Founded in 1997, it is part of the Mondragon Corporation, a cooperative enterprise group, which is a unique feature of our university. This connection provides students with practical learning opportunities and insights into real-world business practices.
