Festivities in Arrasate

Arrasate hosts various local celebrations that showcase the town's rich cultural heritage. Some of the key festivities include:

San Juanak, the village festivals
Maritxu Kajoi, a party where everyone wears fancy clothes
Santamasak, a holiday that marks the beginning of Christmas

Festivities Around the Basque Country

The Basque Country is rich in regional events that attract visitors from all over. Major festivities include:

Semana grande, the main festival of San Sebastian
Euskareren eguna, annual celebration dedicated to the Basque language spoken in the territory of Euskal Herria
