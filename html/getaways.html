Nearby Destinations

Within a couple of hours from Mondragon, you can visit some of the most beautiful cities and landmarks in the Basque Country. Each destination offers a unique experience from historical landmarks to modern attractions. Here are a few recommendations:

Discover Bilbao - A blend of history and modern art with the iconic Guggenheim Museum.
Discover San Sebastián - Known for its beaches and world-class cuisine.
Discover Vitoria-Gasteiz - The green capital of the Basque Country, known for its medieval old town.
Climb to San Juan de Gaztelugatxe - A stunning islet with a scenic path and church at the top.
Walk along Zumaia’s Flysch cliffs - A natural wonder along the coast.
Explore Oñati - A charming village known for its historical architecture and the Sanctuary of Arantzazu.
Visit Zarautz - A surfers' paradise with a long sandy beach.

Getting There

Each of these destinations is easily accessible by bus or train from Mondragon. Plan your trip using Euskotren for regional train connections, or check the Pesa Bus service for buses across the Basque Country.

Nature and Outdoor Activities

If you're an outdoor enthusiast, the Basque Country offers an abundance of options for hiking, cycling, and exploring nature.

Hiking Adventures

The nearby mountains provide incredible views and are perfect for hiking. Some popular routes include:

Aizkorri
Gorbea
Anboto
Udalaitz
  • Aizkorri: One of the most famous peaks in the Basque Country, offering a challenging but rewarding hike with stunning views at the top. The hike often starts from the Sanctuary of Arantzazu. Learn more about Aizkorri.
  • Gorbea: A moderate hike that offers stunning views of the Basque landscape, although it is not the highest peak. Learn more about Gorbea.
  • Anboto: A more challenging hike, known for its dramatic cliffs and cultural significance. Lear more about Anboto.
  • Udalaitz: Located near Mondragon, offering a shorter but equally rewarding hike with panoramic views.

Beaches and Coastal Walks

Looking for a more relaxing activity? Explore the beautiful Basque coastline:

Zarautz
La Concha
Zumaia
Sopelana
  • Zarautz Beach: Popular with surfers, this long stretch of sandy beach is great for a weekend escape. Explore more.
  • La Concha Beach: Located in San Sebastián, famous for its picturesque bay and vibrant atmosphere. Ideal for sunbathing and water sports. Discover La Concha.
  • Flysch Route at Zumaia: A geological wonder, perfect for walking along the cliffs and learning about the region’s history. Flysch Route Info.
  • Playa de Sopelana: A popular beach among locals for surfing and its stunning cliffs. Perfect for a day out and enjoying the sunset. Discover Sopelana.

Travel Tips for Weekend Getaways

  • Plan ahead: Some popular hiking trails and beaches can get crowded, especially during the summer months.
  • Public transport: If you don't have a car, Euskotren and Pesa Bus services are reliable and cover most of the Basque Country.
